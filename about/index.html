I like joining up people and systems, and think technology has a huge role to play in getting people sharing and collaborating.

I'm convinced of the potential of open source, open data and open standards to transform public services, and am a big fan of the platform work being done in GDS. I'd like to see much more of local government organisations working together to solve our common problems.

Much of my work focuses on Multi-Agency working and systems integration, but I'll turn my hand to anything. I also have a particular fondness for replacing people's fax machines and Access databases.

Outside of work I dabble in Ruby, web design and photography. I am still trying to work out how you relax when computers are your hobby as well as your job.
