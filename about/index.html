I like joining up people and systems, and believe technology has a huge role to play in getting people sharing and collaborating.

I think open source, open data and open standards have enormous potential to transform public services, and am a big fan of the platform work being done in GDS. I'd like to see much more of local government organisations working together to solve our common problems.

Much of my current work focuses on Multi-Agency working and systems integration, but I have experience across lots of areas and I'll turn my hand to anything. I also have a particular fondness for replacing people's fax machines and Access databases.

Outside of work I dabble in Ruby, web design and photography. I am still trying to work out how you relax when computers are your hobby as well as your job.
